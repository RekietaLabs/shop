Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how RekietaLabs collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data such as your name, email address, shipping address, and order details when you make a purchase or subscribe to our newsletter.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process orders, deliver products, send order confirmations, and provide updates (if subscribed).

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your data. We share it only with trusted third parties like Stripe (for payments) and shipping providers to fulfill orders.

4. Data Security

We use secure systems and HTTPS to protect your data. Payment information is handled entirely by Stripe and not stored on our servers.

5. Your Rights

You may request to access or delete your data by contacting us at support@rekietalabs.com.

6. Cookies

We may use cookies to improve site functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions, email us at support@rekietalabs.com.
